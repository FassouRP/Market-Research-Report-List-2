<p><p>SMT Inspection Equipment is utilized in the electronics manufacturing industry to ensure the quality of printed circuit boards (PCBs) during the assembly process. It comprises three main types: Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI), which uses cameras to identify defects in PCBs; Solder Paste Inspection (SPI), which assesses the amount and placement of solder paste; and Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI), which captures high-resolution X-ray images to detect faults like component misalignment or solder joint defects. These equipment types play crucial roles in enhancing productivity, reducing errors, and maintaining product quality in PCB assembly.</p></p>
