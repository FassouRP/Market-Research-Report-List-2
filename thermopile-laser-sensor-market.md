<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Thermopile laser sensors are classified into two main types: volume absorber sensors and surface absorber sensors. Volume absorber sensors utilize a material that absorbs the laser light throughout its entire volume, generating a temperature difference that is converted into an electrical signal. On the other hand, surface absorber sensors absorb the laser light only at their surface, converting the temperature difference into an electrical signal. Both types of sensors are used in various applications to measure different laser parameters with high accuracy and reliability.</p></p>
