<p><p>Fluid rotary unions, commonly used in rotating machinery, can be categorized into three different market types based on the number of channels they have. A single channel rotary union allows for the transfer of a single fluid or medium, such as hydraulic oil or air. Double channel rotary unions provide two separate channels for the transfer of different fluids simultaneously. In contrast, multi-channel rotary unions have multiple channels, enabling the transfer of multiple fluids or mediums concurrently, enhancing the overall efficiency and versatility of the equipment.</p></p>
