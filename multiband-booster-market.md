<p><p>The multiband booster market offers various types of boosters designed to enhance cellular signals in different frequency bands. Dual-band boosters amplify signals in two bands, typically 2G and 3G or 4G. Tri-band boosters operate across three bands, such as 2G, 3G, and 4G. Quad-band boosters cover four bands, including all major cellular frequencies. Penta-band boosters support five bands, offering comprehensive signal amplification. Additionally, there are other boosters available that cater to specific frequency requirements, allowing individuals and businesses to choose the most suitable booster for their cellular signal improvement needs.</p></p>
