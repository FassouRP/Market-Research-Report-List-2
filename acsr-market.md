<p><p>ACSR (Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) is an overhead power transmission conductor consisting of a central steel core surrounded by aluminum strands. ACSR/AW (Aluminum Conductor Aluminum-Clad Steel Reinforced) has an aluminum-clad steel core instead of a solid steel core, providing high mechanical strength. ACSR/TW (Aluminum Conductor Trapezoidal-Shaped Steel Reinforced) has a trapezoidal-shaped steel core for improved electrical and mechanical properties. These market types represent different variations of ACSR conductors designed to meet specific requirements for power transmission, such as increased strength and performance in various climates and conditions.</p></p>
