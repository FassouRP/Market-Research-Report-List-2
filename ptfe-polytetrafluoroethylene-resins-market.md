<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) Resins are available in two main types: Dispersion Method and Suspension Method. </p><p>In the Dispersion Method, PTFE resin particles are dispersed in water with the help of surfactants. This method is mainly used to produce fine particles and is suitable for applications where a smooth and even coating is required.</p><p>In the Suspension Method, PTFE resin particles are suspended in a liquid medium, usually hydrocarbon solvents. This method is commonly used for producing large particles and is suitable for applications that require good mechanical strength and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
