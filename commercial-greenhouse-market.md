<p><p>Commercial greenhouses can be divided into two main types: glass greenhouses and plastic greenhouses. They are commonly used for large-scale commercial cultivation of various plants. On the other hand, plastic greenhouses are constructed using plastic materials such as polyethylene or PVC. These greenhouses are more affordable, versatile, and easy to install, making them popular among small-scale or budget-conscious growers. Both types offer controlled environments for optimal plant growth and increased crop yield.</p></p>
