<p><p>Organic LED (OLED) technology has two main market types: PMOLED and AMOLED. PMOLED stands for Passive Matrix OLED, which uses a simpler and cheaper driving method. It is commonly found in small displays and limited resolution applications like wearable devices. On the other hand, AMOLED, or Active Matrix OLED, offers higher resolution and more vibrant colors. It is widely used in larger displays such as smartphones and televisions due to its improved performance and versatility. Both PMOLED and AMOLED contribute to the growing market of OLED technology.</p></p>
