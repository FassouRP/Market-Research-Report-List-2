<p><p>The Pallet Shuttle market consists of two main types: the Standard Pallet Shuttle and the Four-Way Pallet Shuttle. The Standard Pallet Shuttle is designed to move pallets within a warehouse using a roller-based system. It can efficiently transport pallets in and out of racking systems with ease. On the other hand, the Four-Way Pallet Shuttle is equipped with additional capabilities to move pallets in all directions, including sideways. This allows for more flexibility in pallet handling and storage, making it a preferred choice for certain industries and applications.</p></p>
