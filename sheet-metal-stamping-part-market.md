<p><p>Sheet metal stamping is a process used to shape and form metal sheets into various parts. Blanking involves cutting the desired shape from the metal sheet. Embossing creates a raised design on the sheet's surface. Bending alters the shape by manipulating the metal at an angle. Coining produces a defined pattern by compressing the sheet. Flanging forms a raised edge along a straight or curved edge. Other processes like drawing, hemming, and ironing are also commonly used in sheet metal stamping to create different shapes and features on the metal sheet.</p></p>
