<p><p>Thermal-magnetic circuit breakers are commonly used in electrical systems to protect against overloads and short circuits. There are two main types: single-pole and multi-pole. </p><p>Single-pole thermal-magnetic circuit breakers are designed for single-phase applications and can interrupt the flow of current in one circuit. They are commonly used in residential and light commercial settings.</p><p>In contrast, multi-pole thermal-magnetic circuit breakers are designed for three-phase or multi-phase applications, allowing for the interruption of current in multiple circuits simultaneously. They are typically used in larger commercial or industrial settings where multiple circuits need to be protected.</p></p>
