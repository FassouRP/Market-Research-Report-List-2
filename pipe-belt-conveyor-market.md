Stationary pipe belt conveyors are fixed in their position and are commonly used for long-distance transportation. Adjustable pipe belt conveyors can be customized according to the specific needs of the application, allowing for flexibility in movement. Combined pipe belt conveyors combine the features of both stationary and adjustable conveyors, providing a versatile solution for material transportation.</p></p>
