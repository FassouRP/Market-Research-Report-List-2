<p><p>Fiber Optical Coupler market comprises various types of couplers including Y Coupler, T Coupler, Star C Coupler, and Tree Coupler. Y Coupler splits a fiber optic signal into two output signals with equal power. T Coupler divides an input signal into three output signals. Star C Coupler combines multiple inputs and outputs into a single point, efficiently managing signals. Tree Coupler enables multiple input fibers to be split into multiple output fibers, providing flexibility in signal distribution. These different types cater to diverse requirements and applications in the fiber optic industry.</p></p>
