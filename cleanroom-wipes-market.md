<p><p>The cleanroom wipes market can be divided into two types: woven type and non-woven type. Woven type cleanroom wipes are made by weaving together synthetic fibers, resulting in a durable and reusable product. They are commonly used in industries that require high-level cleanliness, such as electronics and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, non-woven type cleanroom wipes are made by bonding fibers together, which makes them disposable. They are often used in industries where convenience and cost-effectiveness are prioritized, such as laboratories and healthcare facilities.</p></p>
