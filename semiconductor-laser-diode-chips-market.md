<p><p>The Semiconductor Laser Diode Chips market can be classified into two types: Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) and Vertical Cavity Surface Emitting Lasers (VCSEL). EELs are designed to emit light from the edge of the chip and are commonly used in applications that require high power output and long-range communication. On the other hand, VCSELs emit light vertically from the chip surface and are preferred in short-range communication systems, facial recognition technology, and optical sensors. Both types play significant roles in various industries, serving different purposes based on their emission characteristics.</p></p>
