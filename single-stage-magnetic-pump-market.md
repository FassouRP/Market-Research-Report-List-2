<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The single-stage magnetic pump market can be categorized into two types based on the shaft design: stationary shaft and rotating shaft. In the stationary shaft market, the impeller and magnet assembly are fixed in position while the power source rotates the outer shell, enabling the pump to function. On the other hand, the rotating shaft market involves a stationary outer shell and a rotating impeller connected to the magnet assembly, which is powered by an external magnetic field. Both types offer efficient and reliable solutions for various industries' pumping needs.</p></p>
