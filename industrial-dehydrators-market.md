<p><p>Industrial dehydrators are used in various industries to remove moisture from different materials, such as food, chemicals, and plastics. There are three types of industrial dehydrators based on their operating principles. Conduction type dehydrators use direct contact with heated surfaces to transfer heat and remove moisture. Convection type dehydrators circulate heated air around the material to evaporate moisture. Radiation type dehydrators use electromagnetic radiation (such as microwaves) to generate heat and remove moisture. Each type offers different advantages and is suitable for specific applications in the industrial dehydrators market.</p></p>
