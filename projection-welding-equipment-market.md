It involves the use of electrodes, typically designed with pointed projections, to create localized heat and pressure at the weld points, thereby fusing the materials together. <p><p>Projection welding equipment market includes three types: manual welding machines, semi-automatic welding machines, and automatic welding machines. </p><p>Manual welding machines require an operator to align the workpiece and electrodes, initiate the weld, and control the weld time. </p><p>Semi-automatic welding machines automate the electrode alignment, but still require manual operation for initiating the weld and controlling the weld time. </p><p>Automatic welding machines fully automate the welding process, including electrode alignment, weld initiation, and weld time control. These machines offer higher productivity and reduced labor costs compared to manual and semi-automatic machines.</p></p>
